# References

This project is based primarily on the following core review paper:

1. **Earl, D.J. & Deem, M.W. (2005).  
   *Parallel Tempering: Theory, Applications, and New Perspectives*.**  
   (The main paper studied and the foundation for this documentation.)

---

## Additional Foundational Works (as cited in the review paper)

These are key historical and methodological papers mentioned inside the review, included here for completeness.

2. Swendsen, R. H., & Wang, J.-S. (1986). Replica Monte Carlo simulation of spin glasses.  
3. Geyer, C. J. (1991). Markov chain Monte Carlo maximum likelihood.  
4. Sugita, Y., & Okamoto, Y. (1999). Replica-exchange molecular dynamics method.  
5. Kofke, D. A. (2002). On the acceptance probability in parallel-tempering Monte Carlo.  
6. Rathore, M., Knotts, T. A., & Kent, M. S. (2005). Optimal allocation of replicas in parallel tempering.  
7. Kone, A., & Kofke, D. A. (2005). Selection of temperature intervals for parallel-tempering simulations.  
8. Whitfield, T. W., Bu, L., & Geissler, P. L. (2002). Tsallis-based parallel tempering methods.  
9. Fukunishi, H., Watanabe, O., & Takada, S. (2002). Hamiltonian parallel tempering for biomolecular sampling.
